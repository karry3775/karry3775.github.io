Product Line Simulation

Full scale design and simulation

  • Visualized the motion and Functions of a Servo-Mechanical Press during a full cycle of deep drawing a cardboard container
  • Improved production cycle of a cardboard-cup production line using event-based multi-body simulation increasing the production rate from 20cups/min to 60cups/min.
  • Programmed the servo-motor cycle using PTC MathCAD to enable a collision control system
